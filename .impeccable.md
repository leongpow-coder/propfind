# Prop Find — Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Singapore property agents evaluating whether to buy a personal landing page from Prop Find. They are busy professionals who spend most of their time on portals like PropertyGuru and 99.co. They arrive at this page with mild scepticism — they need to be convinced quickly that a personal page is worth $20/yr and that it will genuinely change how clients perceive them.

### Brand Personality
Urgent, credible, no-nonsense. Three words: **Sharp. Persuasive. Professional.**

The brand should feel like a confident industry insider talking to a peer — not a flashy tech startup or a generic SaaS product. Every word and visual choice should reinforce that this is a serious business tool that gets results.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Editorial / magazine** — strong typographic hierarchy, bold section contrasts, generous white space punctuated by dense content moments. Think a well-designed trade publication or a boutique consultancy's website. Not corporate stiff, not startup playful — authoritative and precise.

- Light theme throughout, consistent with the main site
- Dark hero/band sections for contrast and urgency
- Primary palette: deep navy (`#1a1a2e`) + crimson accent (`#e94560`)
- Tinted neutrals: warm-cream backgrounds, not pure white or grey
- No glassmorphism, no gradient text, no rounded icon cards
- Typography: pair a distinctive serif display font (Fraunces or Playfair Display) with a clean humanist sans (Plus Jakarta Sans or Outfit) for body
- Accent used sparingly — on CTAs, key numbers, and pull-quote highlights only

### Design Principles
1. **Urgency through contrast** — alternate between light and dark sections to create visual rhythm and maintain forward momentum; never let the eye rest too long
2. **Typography does the work** — hierarchy through size, weight, and spacing; no decoration for its own sake
3. **Social proof is the hero** — the comparison table and reasons grid are the core conversion tools; give them space and visual weight
4. **One action per screen** — every section funnels toward a single WhatsApp CTA; never dilute with competing actions
5. **Motion serves comprehension** — scroll-triggered reveals guide reading order; no animation that doesn't serve the content
